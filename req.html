规则 Rules

  • 你的图必须符合RC，且必须是做完的。这意味着没有正在等待完成的难度，每个难度都已经做完并且下了音效，同时不能使用AI生成或AI辅助。 Your map must follow the RC and must be finished. This means there are no pending diffs, every diff is finished and hitsounded, and AI-generated or AI-assisted content is not allowed.

偏好 Preferences

由于你每隔一段时间才能提交申请一次，所以选择符合我偏好的图提交会增加你的申请被接受的几率。 Since you can only submit once in a while, choosing a map that fits my preferences will increase the chance of your request being accepted.

我喜欢： I like:

  • 能切实表现音乐的有趣的图。 Interesting maps that effectively represent the music.
  • 注重可玩性的图。 Maps that focus on playability.
  • 整洁的图。 Clean maps.

    • 上面这三条是最重要的，比其他加起来都重要。 The three points above are the most important, more than everything else combined.

  • Full Spread，即便在相应时长下RC没有要求必须做低难度。（我鼓励Mapper为新手提供更多选择） Full Spread, even if the RC doesn't require low diffs for that length. (It is encouraged to provide more choices for beginners)
  • 难度名正确指示出实际难度。我不想看到一张4*的Hard或者一张充满180bpm 1/4分离串的Insane。 The diff names accurately represent the actual difficulty. I don't want to see a 4* 'Hard' or an 'Insane' filled with 180bpm 1/4 spacing streams.

我不喜欢： I don't like:

  • 没有真正在表达音乐的、能被称为“XX Slop”的图。 Maps that don't really express the music, often referred to as "XX Slop".
  • 类似于比赛NM2或耐力NM5/DT2的纯串图。切指不在此列。 Pure stream maps similar to tournament NM2, stamina NM5/DT2. Finger control maps are not included in this category.
  • 在所有难度中，有任何一个没有达到我的标准。 Mapset with even one diff that fails to meet my standards.
  • 使用Keysound并且仅仅是在复读原曲（我认为这种Keysound对游戏时的反馈并无帮助，甚至还有负作用）。 Using keysounds that merely repeat the original track (I believe this kind of keysounding doesn't help with gameplay feedback and might even be counterproductive).

    • 上面这些是比较重要的，换句话说，只要你的图足够好足够有趣，下面这些歌曲偏好就不太有所谓。但如果你的图只是还行，那么下面这些就很重要了。 The points above are quite important; in other words, as long as your map is good and interesting enough, the song preferences below don't matter as much. But if your map is just okay, then the following points are very important.

  • 唱功太差的歌手（或者说根本不是歌手）。 Singers with poor singing skills that they can’t even be called singers.
  • 粗制滥造的网红歌曲。 Low-quality viral songs.
  • 较为极端的摇滚。 Relatively extreme rock music.
  • 韩语、俄语歌曲。 Korean or Russian songs.
  • ACG男声歌曲。专业男歌手演唱的流行音乐不在此列。 Anime, game & 2.5D male vocal songs. Pop music performed by professional male singers is not included in this category.
